How to Use the Filter
Plain rules for every scenario
The Rule — Detection Power Decides
⚡
The signal with 80%+ detection power is the one you act on. If the filter has 80%+ power — follow the filter. If only the combo has 80%+ power — follow the combo. If both have 80%+ — follow the filter, it is more specific. If neither has 80% — take at flat risk and log everything.
Take — and at what size
TAKE 1.5x
Filter A+ (80%+) AND Combo USE (80%+), no conflicts
Both signals confirmed and pointing the same way. Maximum conviction. Use 1.5x your normal risk.
TAKE 1.25x
Filter A+ (80%+) — combo not supporting (AVOID, conflict, or below 80%)
Filter has confirmed strong edge on your exact setup. Combo is not adding support but the filter is specific enough to trust on its own. Standard A+ size.
TAKE 1.0x
Filter A (80%+) — any combo reading
Solid confirmed edge. Flat risk. Combo does not change the size at A level.
TAKE 1.0x FLAT
Filter below 80% power — Combo USE (80%+)
Filter has not confirmed this setup yet. Combo is your best available signal and it says take it. Flat risk only until the filter confirms.
Log Only — do not execute
LOG ONLY
Filter AVOID or Below Standard (80%+ power)
Filter has confirmed this setup consistently underperforms. Record what would have happened as a paper trade. Do not execute regardless of the combo reading.
LOG ONLY
Filter below 80% power — Combo AVOID (80%+)
Filter has no confirmed read. Combo is your signal and it says avoid. Record as paper trade.
FLAT RISK — REFERENCE
Neither signal at 80%+ detection power
Not enough data to make a confident call. Take at flat risk if the entry checklist is met and log everything. Most common state before 300 combined trades.
Tag Conflicts
⚠️
A conflict means one of your selected tags is on the combo avoid list. If the filter is A+ confirmed, you still take it at 1.25x — the conflict removes the extra 0.25x from combo support but does not override a confirmed filter. If there is no confirmed filter, the conflict supports the avoid reading.
Always Log
📋
Every valid setup must be logged whether you take it or not. Skipped trades must be recorded as paper outcomes or the dataset loses accuracy over time.
